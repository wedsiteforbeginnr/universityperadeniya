About the University

The University of Peradeniya, located in the beautiful city of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka, is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in the country. It is known for its picturesque campus, academic excellence, and vibrant student life.

Highlights of the University

  • Established in 1942
  • Over 12 faculties offering diverse programs
  • Home to the iconic Peradeniya Botanical Gardens
